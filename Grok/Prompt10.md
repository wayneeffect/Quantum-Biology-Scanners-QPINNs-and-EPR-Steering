- **IT** is the clear giant — it is an umbrella term that includes cloud, software, AI, cybersecurity, etc.
- **AI** (and subsets like **Machine Learning**) punches well above its current size due to massive investment, hype, and transformative potential. It is the highest-value growth engine right now.
- **Cloud Computing** is foundational infrastructure and extremely large.
- **Quantum Computing** is tiny today but represents frontier technology (similar to where AI was ~10–15 years ago).
- **Web Development** is the most "commoditized" and smallest pure-play on this list.
